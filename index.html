Who We Are

Professional tiling services with over 15 years of experience

Oxforce Tiling

Oxforce Tiling is based in Oxford and has been delivering expert tiling services across the UK since 2008. Our experienced tilers handle projects of all sizes, from bathrooms and kitchens to patios and terraces.

We can assist you in sourcing premium tiles and ensure every job is completed with precision and care. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the tiling industry.
